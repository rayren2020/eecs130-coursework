Margery Williams

Margery Williams (1881-1944) was an English-American author beloved for her 1922 classic of children's literature, The Velveteen Rabbit. A professional writer since the age of nineteen, she achieved lasting fame at age forty-one with the publication of The Velveteen Rabbit.
